Child Safety Standards

Age Requirement

Eggus is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a minor, we will delete that information promptly.

Safety Measures

Eggus is committed to providing a safe environment for all users. We maintain the following safety measures to prevent child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) on our platform:

Users can report any inappropriate behavior, content, or suspected abuse directly within the app. All reports are reviewed and actioned promptly.

Users can block any other user at any time, immediately preventing all further interaction.

Eggus complies with all applicable child safety laws and cooperates with law enforcement and relevant authorities when required.

Reporting

If you encounter any content or behavior that violates our safety standards, you can report it in-app or contact us directly at support@eggus.io.

We are committed to responding to all safety reports promptly and taking appropriate action, including account suspension or termination and reporting to the relevant authorities.

Contact

For any questions or concerns about our child safety practices, please contact us at support@eggus.io.
