Introduction

Eggus is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

Information We Collect

We may collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account information (email address, display name)
  • Profile information you choose to share
  • Social media profile information (if you choose to connect via third-party services)
  • Communication data (messages sent through the app)

Location and Device Data

Depending on the features you use, we may collect:

  • Device information and identifiers
  • Location-related data as needed to provide our services
  • Usage data and analytics

You can control what data you share through your device settings and app permissions.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Enable you to discover and connect with nearby users
  • Send you technical notices and support messages
  • Respond to your comments and questions
  • Protect against fraudulent, unauthorized, or illegal activity

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information:

  • With other users as necessary to provide the service (e.g., your display name and avatar)
  • With service providers who assist in operating our app
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • With your consent

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of certain data collection

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
